Greetings! My name is David Chambers and I'm a software developer based in San Francisco.

I work at Atlassian with a great group of people dedicated to making Bitbucket awesome. Our efforts benefit tens of thousands of software developers, which makes the job incredibly rewarding.

I've created several dozen open source projects, many of which are small, self-contained JavaScript utilities written in CoffeeScript. In 2011 I created and released Hashify, a little gift to the Internet. :)
